Fishing British Columbia

The beautiful coastline of BC offers magnificent fishing opportunists, Once you book a cabin room with us you have option of choosing a trolling or fly fishing guided trip. we will be responsible for all licenses and gear required all you need you bring with you is a hunger for adventure!

Dining

We provide a catch and cooking salmon dinner after all of our charters on the water. Our 5 star chefs will prepare your hard earn catch before your very eyes! If you wish to release what we catch on the water we have our own menu to order from, with vegetarian friendly options!

Trolling

We offer a 8 hour full day of trolling in our fleet of comfortable fishing vessels. Each equipped to target Salmon and Halibut at waters near 250 feet deep. We will use tried and true methods using flashers and spoon to target these aggressive fish. Once the fish has been hooked we will set the hook but you will be responsible for fighting and reeling the catch in! you may choose to net the fish in yourself or have the first mate assist.

Fly-Fishing

If you are not a fan of boat fishing and would like something more immersive and challenging we offer world class brown and rainbow trout fly fishing! Grab your boots and waders! We will be walking in the crisp rivers of British Columbia to cast dry flies and nymphs for these elusive fish. Feel the exciting thrill as you strip your fly line and land a trout in your net, as yuo release these rare fish back into the river.
